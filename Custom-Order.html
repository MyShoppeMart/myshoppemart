    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How do i pay?

      You can only pay when your order has been found. This can be done, using our secure online paymnet processing platform. We do not accept payment on delivery for now.

    • What if my order is not found?

      You'd be conttacted asking if you'd want the order cancelled.

    • I've paid, how do i get my product?

      Once your payment is confirmed, you'd be contacted with your delivery details and your items handed over to our dispatch team for delivery.

    • Can i order more than one item at a time?

      Yes! just make sure to clearly differentiate your items with enough details for each of them.

    • Bot Or Form?

      The bot was ideally built for customers on the go, who do not want the hassle of creating an account, on the other hand the form can only be used by registered users.
