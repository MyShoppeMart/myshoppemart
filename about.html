Why My Shoppemart ?

My shoppemart is your non-traditional online and offline shopping, retail and logistics experience and platform that's transforming the way you buy/shop for anything, from anywhere and at anytime, removing the constraints of time and the hassle of distance. we make your shopping, retail and logistics easy.

Mission

Our mission is to enable and give you total control over how you buy/shop for anything, from anytime and at anytime, enabling you explore new possibilities to the marketplace everyplace and power a whole generation of retailers.

Vision

Our vision is to create and lead the next generation and non-traditional(Online and Offline) shopping and logistics experience that takes you away from the hustle and bustle of the marketplace, takes the marketplace to them whilst saving your energy, time and cost.
